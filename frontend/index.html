01

Competing CTAs. Three buttons of equal weight give the reader nothing to decide first. One primary action, always.

02

Wall of text. The subheadline is doing a paragraph's job. Say less, and say it bigger.

03

Low contrast. Light text on a busy background drops below readable contrast on most inboxes.
